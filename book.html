About the Book

This book chronicles my motorcycle journey across all 47 counties of Kenya, capturing the diverse landscapes, rich cultures, and inspiring people that make our nation extraordinary.

Each chapter represents a county, weaving together personal experiences, historical insights, and the lessons learned from thousands of kilometers on the road.
