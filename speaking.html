About Kagwiria

Motorcycle adventurer, storyteller, and motivational speaker currently on a mission to explore all 47 counties of Kenya. Through her journey, Kagwiria has discovered that every mile teaches a lesson, every county tells a story, and every challenge builds character.

Her speaking engagements focus on themes of courage, perseverance, cultural appreciation, and the power of pursuing seemingly impossible dreams.
